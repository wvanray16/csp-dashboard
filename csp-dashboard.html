Greeks, probabilities and the modelers are Black–Scholes estimates from each contract's implied volatility — directional guides, not guarantees. IV percentile and IV/HV compare current at-the-money IV to the stock's realized volatility over the trailing year (a proxy — our data source doesn't serve implied-vol history, so these measure IV against realized vol rather than against past IV). IV percentile is the share of the past year that realized vol sat below today's IV; IV/HV above ~1.15× means options are pricing more movement than the stock has delivered. Calls below your cost basis lock a share loss if assigned (flagged). Positions, holdings & history are saved in this browser. Not investment advice.
